What we help with

Everyday care, clear next steps.

From preventive routines to illness concerns, the clinic focuses on practical treatment, calm explanations and responsible follow-up.

Veterinary consultation

Diagnosis, treatment planning and guidance for common concerns, behavior changes and ongoing health issues.

Vaccination and deworming

Preventive schedules for puppies, adult pets and routine protection, with clear reminders on what comes next.

Wellness checkups

Weight, appetite, skin, coat, digestion and lifestyle checks to catch small problems before they become serious.

Illness care

Support for fever, infections, skin problems, appetite loss, stomach trouble and other everyday medical needs.

Quick answers

Before you visit.

These basics help pet parents act quickly and reduce avoidable stress before a clinic visit.

Should I call before coming?

Yes. Calling first helps confirm doctor availability and gives you immediate guidance for urgent symptoms.

Do you handle vaccination?

Yes. The clinic supports vaccination and deworming schedules for preventive pet care.

Can I ask about street animals?

Yes. PetVet supports community animal care whenever possible and can guide you on first steps.
